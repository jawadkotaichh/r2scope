What you should focus on first

If your goal is understand the repo enough to reproduce and then modify it, I would focus in this order:

src/config/algs/rode.yaml
src/main.py
src/run.py
src/runners/episode_runner.py
src/controllers/rode_controller.py
src/learners/rode_learner.py
src/modules/agents/rode_agent.py and src/modules/roles/dot_role.py
src/envs/starcraft2/starcraft2.py



main.py loads configs and seeds 
→ run.py builds runner/buffer/controller/learner 
→ episode_runner.py collects episodes from SC2 
→ rode_controller.py selects roles and actions 
→ rode_learner.py samples batches and trains Q-functions/mixers/action encoder
→ checkpoints get saved in results/models

python3 src/main.py --config=rode --env-config=sc2 with env_args.map_name=3m t_max=2050000
